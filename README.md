## What

- Drag to pan
- Scroll to zoom
- Mess with the sliders
- Upload a MP3 and press play
- Upload a custom font
- Record a GIF

Note: no uploaded file (audio or font) are retained, as they technically stay on your computer.

## With

- [p5.js](https://p5js.org/) including p5-sound
